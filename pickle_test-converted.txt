 Pickle, esq.; the father of that hero whose fortunes we propose to relate.To record.ied before he could see his son in possession of it

He was the son of a merchant in London. His father had raised himself to the highest honours of the city and acquired a plentiful fortune. However, he died before he could see his son in possession of it.He worked hard before he became successful, telling his son to work hard and follow his example until he made up for his own lack of success.This was a sum of considerably less than fifteen thousand pounds.
