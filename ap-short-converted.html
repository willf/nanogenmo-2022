Against Praxeas

1 The devil has shown hostility to the Truth in many ways.He has tried to defend it at times, but he's only pretending.He is the champion of the one Lord, the Almighty, the creator of the world, so that he may make a heresy out of the unity.He says that the Father himself came down into the virgin, himself was born of her, himself suffered, in short himself is Jesus Christ.- The serpent has forgotten himself: for when he tempted Jesus Christ after the baptism of John it was as Son of God that he attacked him, being assured that God has a son at least from those very scriptures which speak of the serpent as the tempter.He was creating the temptation from different aspects. If you are the Son of God, command that these stones be made bread. : again, shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee, and in their hands they shall bear thee up, lest at any time thou dash thy foot against a stone. If you are the Son of God, throw yourself down from here. It is written that he will give his angels charge over you, to keep you, and they will bear you up in their hands, so that you will not dash your foot against a stone. The father, of course. God has given his angels orders to take care of you and keep you from stumbling. Or will he accuse the gospels of lying, and say, "Let Matthew and Luke see to it: I for my part approached God himself, I tempted the Almighty hand to hand: that was the reason for my approach, that wager was my stake, and I won it."as the reason for the temptation: otherwise, if it had been only God's son, perhaps I should not have demeaned myself to tempt him"?No, he himself is a liar from the beginning, and anyone he has paid to lie for him, like Praxeas.his having been the first to confess. This person was the first to import to Rome out of Asia a wrongheadedness-a man generally of restless character, and moreover puffed up with boasting of his confessorship on account of his having been the first to confess.Although he would have profited nothing if he had given his body to be burned, he would have only experienced a short discomfort of imprisonment.also drove out by force.At that time, the bishop of Rome was about to recognize the prophecies of Montanus and Prisca and Maximilla, and as a result, was offering peace to the churches of Asia.tered men and to excommunicate the rest This man made false claims about the prophets and their churches, and insisted on the decisions of the bishop's predecessors. Because of this, the bishop was forced to recall the lettered men and excommunicate the rest.The terms of peace have already been issued, so please stop trying to receive spiritual gifts.Praxeas did two things that pleased the devil: he got rid of prophecy and introduced false teaching. He chased away the Holy Spirit and put Jesus to death.Weeds were planted among the wheat, and they have also sprouted here, while many people are asleep in their naivete.After that, they were revealed by whoever God wanted, and it seemed like they had been completely removed.The teacher gave up his old opinions and promised to obey the new rules. His promise is kept by the people who were there when he made it.After that, no one said anything.I was separated from the natural men by my acknowledgement and defence of the Paraclete.But those weeds had at that time scattered their seed everywhere, and so for a time it lay hidden, deceptively pretending to be dead, and has now burst forth anew.But it will also be replanted if the Lord wills, in the time I have now: if not, then in its due time all counterfeit grain will be gathered and burned.He will be punished with unquenchable fire, along with other offenses.
